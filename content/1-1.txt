['pə:kəleit]
写
v.

verb

【释义】
[I]  if an idea, feeling, or piece of information percolates through a group, it gradually spreads

The message has begun to percolate through the organization.
那消息开始在整个机构中传了开来。

These ideas were slow to percolate.
这些观念流传得很慢。

〔液体、光或空气〕渗透，滤出:
【例句】
Rainwater percolates down through the rock.
雨水顺着岩石的裂缝渗漏下去。
3.
【释义】
[I,T]  also  perk if coffee percolates, or if you percolate it, you make it in a special pot in which hot water goes up through a tube and then passes down through crushed coffee beans
